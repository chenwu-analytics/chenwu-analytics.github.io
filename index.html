Tokenised Assets Risk Framework

Systemic Stablecoin Model

A Python-based quantitative stress-testing model aligned with the latest FCA/BoE regulatory updates (June 2026), simulating £1B systemic liquidity under SVB/Terra-style panic runs.

Risk Evaluation

Analysed the mismatch between 24-hour customer redemption mandates and the T+1 settlement lag of selling government bonds. Conducted an extreme boundary stress test by applying the regulators' maximum 'step-up' limits (95% Gilts / 5% Cash) to evaluate survival against massive Day-1 bank runs.

Commercial Insights
  • Dynamic Cash Buffers: Voluntarily expanding unremunerated cash to 10-15% absorbs Day-1 shockwaves, mitigating the fatal 95% regulatory ceiling.
  • Private Repo Lines: Pre-arranged Repo lines provide cheaper emergency cash and prevent the market panic of a central bank bailout.
  • Capital Adequacy: Firms must hold extra equity to cover the 1% BoE haircut; otherwise, pledged Gilts won't generate enough cash.
  • BoE Pre-integration: Selling Gilts takes T+1 days. Without BoE access, a 24-hour redemption mandate mathematically guarantees a default.
